Welcome to Our ASL Letter and Digit Recognition Website

This is a dedicated webpage for an American Sign Language (ASL) Recognition Machine. To Begin, please press the button below and take a picture of your hand forming a letter or digit handsign in the ASL alphabet. Our model was based on a paper from Stanford University that used transfer learning with a GoogLeNet architecture and was trained/tested on the following datasets:

  • Massey University Gesture Dataset 2012
  • University of Exeter ASL Finger Spelling Dataset
